🔥 Fire Hydrant Rescue 🔥

Oh no! Fires are spreading across the city!
Your mission: stop the fires with your trusty fire hydrant cannon.

How to play:

  • PC: Arrow keys to move, SPACE to shoot water
  • Mobile: Tap arrows to move, 💧 button to spray water

Press SPACE (or tap anywhere) to start!
